Colour Palette

rgb(5, 27, 38)– Darkblue

 •  used for the overall background color.

rgb(143,235,217) – Lightblue

 •  used to highlight the item that is being hovered over.

rgb(3, 6, 20) – Black

 •  used for the header background and buttons.

rgb(255,255,255) – White

 •  used for overall text, makes great contrast to the background color.

Typefaces

 •  The main font is sans-serif Verdana, Geneva, Tahoma. Geneva is a neo-grotesque or "industrial" sans-serif typeface designed by Susan Kare for Apple Computer. It is one of the oldest fonts shipped with Macintosh operating systems.
